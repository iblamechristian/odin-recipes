Pizza

Description

This pizza will make you go "Oh my fucking god this shit is so FUCKing delicious. The cheese is the combination of all the perfect blends focusing their deliciousness through your body. The Sauce is a hot wet towel that makes you feel warm and safe. The pepperoni is the perfect topping to add a joyful glow to the dish. To finish it off, the perfect herbs and seasonings, designed to tie the whole recipe together.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Your choice of seasonings

Steps

  1. Stretch dough into desired size
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the dough, leaving room for the crust
  3. Place cheese on top of pizza sauce
  4. Optional: Place pepperoni on the cheese
  5. Toss pizza in the oven at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes
  6. Enjoy!
